  • Full-length transcripts: CCS sequences containing 5'-primer, 3'-primer and poly-A.

  • Transcript de-redundancy:Two or more third-generation sample data were combined and de-redundant, and then were transcribed using CD-HIT software (parameters: -c 0.95 -T 6 -G 0 -aL 0.90 -AL 100 -aS 0.99 -AS 30).For this cluster analysis, the comparative analysis of transcripts here was based on sequence similarity (>95%) and was not related to transcript expression levels.

  • CDS prediction: Starting with the three coding reading boxes in the forward direction, there are three possibilities, that is, reading and translating from the first to the third base in the forward direction (labeled 0,1,2). According to the prediction results, multiple CDS sequences (amino acid length ≥50) may be output in the end, and the CDS sequence with the longest transcript is selected as the best prediction result.

  • Shared transcripts:When there are less than 2 third-generation samples, it's not possible to do the analysis of shared transcripts and the corresponding venn diagram.
